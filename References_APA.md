- **Journal Articles:** Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Journal, volume*(issue), page-page. DOI or URL

- **Conference Papers:** Author, A. A. (Year). Title of paper. In *Proceedings of Conference Name* (pp. page-page). DOI or URL

- **Books:** Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work*. Location: Publisher.

- **Preprints:** Author, A. A. (Year). Title of preprint. *arXiv preprint arXiv:xxxx.xxxxx*. Retrieved from URL

- **Web Resources:** Author/Organization. (Year). Title of page. Retrieved from URL
